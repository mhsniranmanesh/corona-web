Stay informed

With a communication chain -with people you know, or even don't know directly- created by this application, you will be informed if you or any of your closed contacts are infected, or have a high risk of infection.
All you need to do, is adding your closed contacts and answering disease assessments. Hence you will be notified if you or your relatives need medical care or prevention, and help people around the world has more information.

An app for Each
& Everyone

Users can update their information as symptoms change and these changes are reflected on the map and radar so you can have enough information about all the places virus situation and manage your commuters.
We have 3 different categories in the Coronatic app:
Users that have not tested positive for the virus and are asymptomatic are shown as Green Point.
Users that have not tested positive for the virus but have symptoms such as a fever, cough or shortness of breath are shown as Yellow Point.
Users who self-report that they have tested positive for the corona virus are shown as Red Point.

Disease assessment

Map

Radar

Using Radar option allows you to track the epidemic in real-time. users will find the 'Real-time Epidemic Surveillance Around You. The data are about the number of confirmed coronavirus cases in that local area and about the risk factors of that region. Details about the nearest isolation camps and healthcare facilities and hospitals are also available in this feature
